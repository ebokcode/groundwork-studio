Questions Answered

Financing FAQ

What credit score do I need to qualify?
We work with multiple financing partners to accommodate a wide range of credit scores. While the 0% interest plans typically require fair-to-good credit, our low monthly plan options are available to a broader range of applicants. Apply and see — there's no cost to check.
Does applying affect my credit score?
The initial application typically uses a soft credit inquiry which does not affect your credit score. A hard inquiry may be performed if you proceed with the financing, which has a minimal, temporary impact on your score.
Can I pay off my balance early?
Yes. There are no prepayment penalties. You can pay off the full balance at any time. If you're on the 0% plan, paying it off before the promotional period ends means you pay zero interest.
What happens if I don't pay off the 0% balance in time?
If the promotional balance isn't paid off by the end of the 0% period, deferred interest may apply from the original purchase date. We recommend setting up automatic payments to avoid this. Your financing documents will include the full terms.
