Our Story

About Desert
Ridge Auto.

Founded in 2008, Desert Ridge Auto has been Mesa's trusted family-owned repair shop for over 16 years. What started as a two-bay operation has grown into a full-service facility — without ever losing the values that built our reputation: honesty, quality workmanship, and standing behind every job.

Every technician on our team is ASE certified, and we've serviced over 4,000 vehicles across Mesa, Gilbert, and Chandler. We treat every car like it belongs to a neighbor — because most of the time, it does.
