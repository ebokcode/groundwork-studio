An Exclusive Experience

The Chef's Table

Seats six to eight guests in an intimate kitchen-facing dining space. Chef Marcus Webb presents a curated seven-course tasting menu designed entirely around the week's finest arrivals from our farm partners.

The Chef's Table is offered Friday and Saturday evenings only, by reservation. The experience includes beverage pairing options and a personal introduction to each course from Chef Webb or his sous chef.

Courses 7-course seasonal tasting
Seating 6–8 guests maximum
Availability Friday & Saturday only
Price $185 per person · Pairings +$75
Lead time 72-hour advance booking required
