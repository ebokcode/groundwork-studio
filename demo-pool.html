What We Build

Crafted for the Arizona lifestyle

Every Blue Horizon project begins with a vision and ends with something that exceeds it. We handle design, permitting, construction, and ongoing care — all under one roof.

01 — Service

Custom Pool
Design

Your pool should reflect your home and your lifestyle — not a catalog template. We design every pool from scratch: shape, depth, finishes, lighting, and landscape integration.

  • 3D renderings before a single permit is filed
  • Freeform, geometric & infinity edge designs
  • Pebblesheen, quartz & glass tile finishes
  • Smart automation & LED lighting systems
Explore Service →

02 — Service

Pool
Renovation

Dated plaster, cracked coping, tired tile — we bring aging pools back to life. A renovation can transform a neglected backyard into your most-used room in the house.

  • Full replaster & remodel packages
  • Coping, decking & tile replacement
  • Equipment upgrades to variable-speed pumps
  • Adding waterfalls, sun shelves & spas
Explore Service →

03 — Service

Spa & Water
Features

Elevate the experience. From integrated spillover spas and grottos to fire bowls and sheer descents — we design water features that make your pool unforgettable.

  • Attached & detached custom spas
  • Grottos, rock formations & waterslides
  • Fire & water feature combinations
  • Sheer descents, scuppers & bubblers
Explore Service →

04 — Service

Pool
Maintenance

Your pool deserves the same care that went into building it. Our certified technicians handle weekly service, equipment repair, and seasonal prep so you never lift a finger.

  • Weekly chemical balancing & cleaning
  • Filter, pump & heater repair & replacement
  • Algae treatment & water clarity guarantee
  • Annual inspection & equipment report
