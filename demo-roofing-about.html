How We Started

A Third-Generation Roofer With a Point to Prove

Peak Shield Roofing was founded in 2012 by Marcus Rivera, a third-generation roofer who grew up working summers on job sites with his father and grandfather. After 8 years working for large roofing contractors, Marcus started Peak Shield with one truck and a simple belief: homeowners deserve honest assessments, quality materials, and a crew that treats their home with respect.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a team of 14 skilled tradespeople serving the entire Denver metro area. We've completed over 500 roofs, earned a 4.8-star rating across Google and Yelp, and built a reputation that keeps neighbors referring neighbors.

We're still the same company Marcus started — local, honest, and accountable. Every project gets the same attention whether it's a single-square repair or a full commercial reroof. And we still back every job with our 100% cleanup guarantee, because a job isn't done until your yard is spotless.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🤝
Honesty First

We tell you what you actually need — not what makes us the most money. If your roof can be repaired instead of replaced, we'll say so. Our long-term business depends on trust, not upsells.

🏗️
Quality Materials Only

We install GAF, Owens Corning, and CertainTeed — no off-brand substitutes, no discounted seconds. Every material we use comes with manufacturer backing, and our installs meet or exceed manufacturer specs.

🌄
Community Built

We're Denver-based and locally owned. We hire locally, buy locally, and donate 1% of our annual profits to Colorado housing nonprofits. When Peak Shield does well, we try to make Denver do better.
