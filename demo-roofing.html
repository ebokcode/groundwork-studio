Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need a full replacement or just a repair?
If your roof is over 20 years old, has extensive missing shingles, or has active leaks in multiple spots, replacement is usually the better investment. We'll tell you honestly which one you actually need after our free inspection.
What roofing materials do you use?
We work with GAF, Owens Corning, CertainTeed, and other top manufacturers. We'll recommend the best material for your climate, budget, and home style.
How long does a roof replacement take?
Most residential replacements are done in 1–2 days. Larger or more complex roofs may take 3–4 days.
Do you handle permits and HOA approvals?
Yes. We pull all required city and county permits and will handle HOA submissions if needed.
What warranty do you offer?
We offer up to a 30-year workmanship warranty on all installations, plus manufacturer warranties up to 50 years on qualifying materials.
Do you offer financing?
Yes. We partner with GreenSky and other lenders to offer $0 down, 0% interest financing on approved credit. Ask about options at your inspection.
