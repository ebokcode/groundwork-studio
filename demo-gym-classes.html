Meet the Coaches

Your Instructors

Marcus Webb

NASM-CPT · HIIT & Strength Specialist · 9 Years

Marcus is the energy behind our HIIT and Strength programs. A former college athlete turned certified coach, he specializes in performance-based training and has helped hundreds of members hit PRs they never imagined. His sessions are intense, precise, and — somehow — fun.

Elena Reyes

ACE-CPT · Spin & Cycling Certified · 7 Years

Elena's spin classes have a cult following — members drive across the valley just to make her 6 AM class. She blends rhythm, endurance, and motivation in a way that makes 50 minutes on a bike feel like the best part of your week. Certified in indoor cycling and sports nutrition.

Priya Shah

RYT-500 · Pilates Certified · 11 Years

Priya brings a calming presence and incredible technical depth to every Yoga and Pilates class. With training in Iyengar, Vinyasa, and restorative traditions, she meets every student where they are — whether you're recovering from injury or preparing for peak athletic performance.
