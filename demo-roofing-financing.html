FAQ

Financing Questions Answered

Do I need good credit to qualify?
Not necessarily. We work with multiple lending partners who consider a range of credit profiles. While approval isn't guaranteed, we encourage homeowners with all credit backgrounds to apply — you may be surprised by what's available.
Will applying affect my credit score?
Checking your rate requires only a soft credit pull, which has no impact on your score. A hard pull only occurs if you choose to move forward with a specific loan offer and accept the terms.
How long does the application take?
The application itself takes under 5 minutes to complete online. Decisions come back in seconds in most cases. Once approved, we can typically schedule your project within a few business days.
Can I pay off my loan early?
Yes. Our extended payment plans have no prepayment penalty. You're free to pay off your balance early at any time without any additional fees or charges.
