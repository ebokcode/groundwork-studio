Our Story

Rooted in the desert,
nourished by craft.

Copper & Sage Kitchen was born from a simple conviction — that great food starts long before it reaches the kitchen. We partner with more than a dozen local Arizona farms, ranches, and purveyors to build a menu that changes with the seasons and honors what grows here.

Executive Chef Mara Sandoval spent a decade cooking through the kitchens of San Francisco and New York before returning to the Southwest with a clear purpose: to bring the editorial precision of fine dining to the honest flavors of the Sonoran table.
