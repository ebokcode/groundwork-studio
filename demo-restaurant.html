Our Story

Where the Sonoran Desert
Meets the Harvest Table

Copper & Sage Kitchen was born from a simple belief: the best meal is one that knows where it came from. Nestled in the heart of Scottsdale, our kitchen draws from a network of Arizona farms and ranches — transforming seasonal harvests into refined, unforgettable plates.

Executive Chef Marcus Webb and his team craft each dish with the integrity of the farmer and the artistry of the craftsman. Every service is a celebration of place, season, and connection.
