The Process

What to Expect

📋

Assessment First

Every new PT client starts with a full movement screen, body composition analysis, and goal-setting session. We build from your baseline — not a template.

🎯

Custom Programming

Your trainer writes a program specific to you — periodized training blocks, progressive overload, and built-in recovery. Delivered to your app weekly.

📈

Tracked Progress

Monthly re-assessments, photo check-ins if you opt in, and detailed strength tracking so you always know exactly how far you've come.
