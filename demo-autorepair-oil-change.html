How Often?

Oil Change Interval Guide

Arizona's extreme heat accelerates oil degradation. Follow these guidelines — and always consult your owner's manual or ask our techs.

3,000
Conventional

Older vehicles and high-mileage engines may benefit from more frequent changes. Best for vehicles older than 10 years.

5,000
Conventional — AZ Recommended

In Arizona's heat, conventional oil breaks down faster. We recommend 5,000 miles max for most gas engines using conventional oil.

7,500
Full Synthetic

Full synthetic oil holds up better in extreme temperatures. Most modern vehicles do well on a 7,500–10,000 mile synthetic schedule.

Our Process

Done Right,
Every Time

Our technicians follow a precise 4-step process to make sure every oil change is completed correctly and quickly.

01
Drive In, Check In

Pull into our service bay and our advisor will greet you, confirm your oil type preference, and get your vehicle into the queue — no wait, no paperwork hassle.

02
Drain & Replace

We safely lift your vehicle, drain the old oil, replace the filter, and refill with fresh oil to the manufacturer's exact specification.

03
Multi-Point Inspection

While the oil is draining, your technician walks through our 21-point checklist — tires, brakes, battery, fluid levels, belts, and lights.

04
Review & Roll

We walk you through the inspection results, answer any questions, and you're on your way. Most oil changes done in 45 minutes or less.

Questions Answered

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does an oil change take?
Most oil changes are completed in 30–45 minutes. If we find something during the inspection that needs attention, we'll let you know before doing any additional work.
Do I need an appointment?
No appointment needed for oil changes — we accept walk-ins Monday through Saturday. However, calling ahead or booking online guarantees your preferred time slot and can reduce wait time.
What brand of oil do you use?
We stock Mobil 1, Castrol, Pennzoil, and Valvoline full synthetic and conventional oils. We always use the viscosity grade specified by your vehicle manufacturer. Just ask if you have a preference.

No appointment needed. Walk in Monday–Saturday or book online in under 2 minutes.
